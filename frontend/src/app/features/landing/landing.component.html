Core modules

Six modules. One project record.

Every module reads and writes to the same project, so a change on the schedule shows up in the budget, and a delivery logged in inventory shows up on the site engineer's task list.

MOD-01

Project Scheduling

Build phased timelines, set milestones, and see which tasks are blocking the next crew from starting.

MOD-02

Workforce & Crews

Assign project managers, engineers, contractors and workers to specific sites, shifts and trades.

MOD-03

Materials & Inventory

Track stock by site, flag low supply automatically, and route procurement requests to the right approver.

MOD-04

Equipment & Maintenance

See which machinery is on which site, and get ahead of maintenance before it causes downtime.

MOD-05

Budget & Cost Control

Compare committed, actual and forecast spend against the original estimate, line by line.

MOD-06

Daily Reports & Photos

Site engineers log progress and site conditions from a phone; clients see a clean summary, not the raw feed.

Team roles

Everyone sees only what their job needs

Access is scoped by role, not by trust. Each person opens a dashboard built around what they're responsible for.

Full access

Administrator

Manages user accounts, permissions and platform-wide settings.

Project-level

Project Manager

Builds the schedule, allocates crews and materials, and signs off on budget changes.

Site-level

Site Engineer

Submits daily progress, inspection results and site condition reports.

Task-level

Contractor

Executes assigned work packages and manages their own field team.

Task-level

Worker

Clocks attendance and marks assigned tasks complete from a phone.

Read-only

Client

Follows project status and progress reports without seeing internal costs.
